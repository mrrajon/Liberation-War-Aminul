Ahmed Rahim

Freedom Fighter (Sector 2)

Testimony Summary:

Mr. Rahim recounts his experiences training in Agartala and participating in guerrilla operations in Dhaka. He details a specific operation targeting a supply convoy in November 1971 and reflects on the morale of the freedom fighters during the final month of the war.

Professor Anisuzzaman

Educator and Intellectual

Testimony Summary:

In this video interview, Professor Anisuzzaman discusses the role of intellectuals in shaping Bengali nationalism leading up to 1971. He shares personal memories of the crackdown on March 25th and his subsequent journey crossing the border to join the planning cells of the provisional government.

Fatema Begum

War Heroine / Nurse in Field Hospital

Testimony Summary:

Mrs. Begum shares harrowing and inspiring stories of treating wounded freedom fighters in makeshift field hospitals near the border. She speaks about the resilience of local communities in providing shelter and food to the Mukti Bahini despite the immense risks.
